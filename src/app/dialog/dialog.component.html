Commands

Control Robert the Robot while he moves around a 5x5 grid! Begin by using PLACE X,Y,F

PLACE X,Y,F: will put Robert on the table in position X,Y and facing NORTH, SOUTH, EAST or WEST
MOVE: will move Robert one unit forward in the direction it is currently facing
LEFT and RIGHT: will rotate the robot 90 degrees in the specified direction without changing the position of the robot
REPORT: will announce the X,Y and F of the robot
